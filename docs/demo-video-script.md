# Demo Video Script

**Target Length**: 60–90 seconds
**Format**: Screen recording with voiceover (or text overlay)

---

## Opening (0:00 – 0:10)

**Show**: Dashboard home page loading
**Say**: "This is SaaSBoard — a business operations dashboard I built with Next.js, TypeScript, and Tailwind CSS."

## Dashboard Overview (0:10 – 0:25)

**Show**: Scroll through KPI cards, hover over charts, scan activity feed
**Say**: "The dashboard home gives you an at-a-glance view of your key metrics — revenue trends, account growth, recent activity, and active alerts. Everything updates from a mock data layer designed to mirror real API contracts."

## Accounts (0:25 – 0:40)

**Show**: Navigate to Accounts, type in search, filter by status, click a row to open detail drawer
**Say**: "The accounts page features a searchable, filterable data table with status badges, subscription tiers, and a slide-over detail panel. Each account has realistic data — industry, owner, MRR, and activity history."

## Workflows (0:40 – 0:50)

**Show**: Navigate to Workflows, click through status filters, show task cards
**Say**: "The workflow view tracks tasks with priority levels, due dates, assignees, and project tags. The health summary gives a quick overview of team progress."

## Billing (0:50 – 1:00)

**Show**: Navigate to Billing, scroll through metrics, chart, and invoices
**Say**: "Billing shows MRR, plan distribution, top accounts by revenue, and a full invoice table — everything a finance team needs at their fingertips."

## Settings (1:00 – 1:10)

**Show**: Navigate to Settings, click through tabs (Organization, Notifications, Integrations)
**Say**: "The settings area includes organization config, notification preferences, integration management, and security controls — all with realistic form patterns."

## Closing (1:10 – 1:20)

**Show**: Quick scroll through mobile view
**Say**: "The entire app is responsive, type-safe, and built with a scalable component architecture. It's ready for real API integration, authentication, and production deployment. Built by [Your Name]."

---

## Production Notes

- Record at 1440x900 desktop resolution
- Use smooth, deliberate mouse movements
- Keep transitions snappy — don't linger
- Background music: subtle, professional (optional)
- Export at 1080p
